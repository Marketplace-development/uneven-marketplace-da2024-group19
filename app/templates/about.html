About Us

At Woofwatchers, we bring dog owners and sitters together to ensure quality care for every dog. While the sitters handle the services, we focus on verifying their reliability and providing 24/7 support for any issues that arise. With us, your dog’s safety and happiness are always our top priority.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a trusted community where dog lovers support each other. We want to give dog owners peace of mind by connecting them with dedicated sitters who guarantee safety, love, and care.
